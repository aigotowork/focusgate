Built for these boundary moments

FocusGate is not a punishment tool and does not claim to be impossible to bypass. It carries decisions made while you are clear-headed into moments when distraction becomes easier.

Put the screen down before sleep

The default sleep rule group can limit video, social, news, and other high-stimulation sites at night.

Protect work focus

Create weekday rule groups so entertainment sites and feeds are harder to open during important hours.

Reset digital habits

Use reminders, block pages, and unlock friction to give weekends or rest days back to slower real life.

Local-first privacy design

The current MVP does not provide accounts, cloud sync, or user-data sales. Rule groups, restricted domains, temporary unlocks, and stats are stored locally by default. The extension does not read page body text, form inputs, account passwords, or page titles.
